Spring Sale 2025

It's springtime for discounts!

Spring is here. No matter what that pesky thermometer says! And as I crave sunshine, I'm deciding it's absolutely spring which means it's also time for our Spring Sale (no matter if you still need to wear mittens as you venture outside) starting on April 8 10am PT and stretching until April 22 10am PT.

The sale is packed with over 200 discounted pieces of content starting at 33% off. There are also 10 Character Creator items with a 33% discount, which you'll find in the Dressing Room!

So, put on your shorts, apply some sunscreen, and frolic in all this lush greenness (I promise, just squint a bit) and see what budding offers are braving the permafrost ground.

Doorbusters

Doorbusters are just like those flowers that are ever-adamant that it's their time now and that winter simply must take a chill-pill! Basically, they are items that are 75% off, with two new ones blooming every day of the sale!

Here are some of my favorites:

Mash-up: RealismCraft's Let My Spark Universe

World: Wolf Life by Flint Studios

Shedding your winter fur and howling at the moon... is there anything more spring-like than that? Especially when you've transformed into an actual wolf! In Wolf Life, you're not just any old wolf, but an ice wolf, fire wolf, or perhaps a Sakura wolf? Take on quests and unleash your ultimate wolf powers to celebrate spring, lycanthrope style!

Add-on: Skibidi Toilet Raids by LuganTzip

Besides crocuses and tulips, the absolute peak sign that spring is here is the wonderful sight of walking toilets with heads raiding entire villages complete with that very spring-esque feel of orchestrated chaos in the fresh, crisp air (I'm almost getting teary-eyed just thinking about it). In Skibidi Toilet Raids, you'll fight endless waves of Skibidi Toilets, upgrade your Cameramen, and much more. This is exactly the type of blossoming spring mayhem I crave as the snow melts!
